# **App Name**: Shree Engineers - Land Surveying

## Core Features:

- Homepage Introduction: Display a visually appealing home page with a brief introduction to Shree Engineers and its history since 1998.
- Project Portfolio: Showcase completed land surveying and development projects with images and descriptions.
- Services Overview: Clearly outline the land surveying and development services offered by Shree Engineers.
- Quote Request Form: Implement a 'Request a Quote' form for potential clients to submit their project details.
- Contact Information: Provide contact information, including phone number, email, and a contact form for inquiries.
- AI-Powered Quote Responder: A tool to auto-generate tailored email responses to quote requests, including relevant services and project examples.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Blue (#1E3A8A) to evoke trust and professionalism.
- Background color: Light Gray (#F5F5F5) for a clean and modern look.
- Accent color: Earthy Orange (#C2670C) to convey stability and history.
- Body and headline font: 'Literata', a transitional serif that looks credible and professional, to reflect the firm's experience since 1998.
- Use clean, professional icons related to surveying and engineering.
- Maintain a structured and organized layout with clear navigation.
- Incorporate subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience.
